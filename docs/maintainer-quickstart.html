Branch Mailer — Maintainer Quickstart

One page. Print it or keep it pinned — everything a maintainer needs day to day.

Add someone from the GAL to a group

  1. Open Branch Mailer → tap Manage Groups (top right — only visible to maintainers).
  2. Pick the group from the dropdown.
  3. Type their name into the Search GAL by name box.
  4. Tap their name in the results list. They're added straight away — no save button needed.

Add someone who isn't in the GAL (external/contractor)

  1. Same screen, same group selected.
  2. Under Add manually, type their full name and email address.
  3. Tap Add.

Remove someone from a group

  1. Pick the group from the dropdown.
  2. Find their row in the member list.
  3. Tap the bin icon next to their name. They're removed straight away.

Removing someone only takes them out of that one group. If they're in other groups too, those are untouched.

Add a brand new group

This one step can't be done from inside the app — it needs a quick trip to the SharePoint list directly:

  1. Go to the BM Groups list on the branch SharePoint site.
  2. Click + New, type the group name in the Title field.
  3. Leave Active as Yes.
  4. Tick ThursdayGroup only if this group should be included when the Thursday preset button is used.
  5. Save. It appears in the app straight away (close and reopen the app if it doesn't show immediately).

Retire a group without losing its history

Don't delete the group row. Instead open it in BM Groups and set Active to No. It disappears from the app immediately, but its members and the list's version history are kept.

Who can do all this?

Only the people listed in the BM Maintainers list. To add another maintainer, a site owner adds a row there (name + email) — nothing in the app itself needs to change.
